Our Mission

Want to eat healthy?

The simple choice is to go with NouriGO (Nourish and Go) , a healthy alternative to junk food.

Problem:

  • According to NYC health, in New York City obesity is an epidemic. More than half of adult New Yorkers are overweight (34%) or obese (22%).
  • There are short term and long term ramifications towards the unhealthy food options that are solely available to the demographics of working adults and college students.
  • According to the CDC, “Perceived barriers to fruit and vegetable consumption include cost, as well as limited availability and access (6–8).”

Solution:

  • NouriGO makes healthy and affordable food far more accessible compared to other brick and mortar locations customers would have to travel to.
  • Our company would be in locations near CUNY college campuses and low income neighborhoods where there is a demand for healthy well-priced food options but is unavailable.
