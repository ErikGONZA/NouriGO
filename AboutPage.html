Our Mission

According to NYC health, in New York City obesity is epidemic. More than half of adult New Yorkers are overweight (34%) or obese (22%). As New Yorkers, we are almost never out of options when it comes to finding the nearest McDonald’s or Halal Cart. Quick access to cheap, fast food options has become a public health issue as healthier options are inconvenient, costly and simply just hard to find.

We target New York City’s college students and working parents, starting an affordable healthy food truck may be the solution to our problems. Our trucks can specialize in vegan styled grilled chicken wraps and paninis that are not only affordable but also taste really good. In addition, it will be located in several hot spots around the city that are just as accessible as fast food restaurants. This will make a choice to eat healthy an easier one.
